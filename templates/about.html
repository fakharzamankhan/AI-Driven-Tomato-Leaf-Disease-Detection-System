About AI Tomato Care

What is AI Tomato Care?

AI Tomato Care is a web application that helps farmers, growers, and home gardeners identify tomato leaf diseases from photos. Using a trained deep learning model, the platform can classify Early Blight, Late Blight, Leaf Mold, or a Healthy leaf condition. After each scan, users receive practical care guidance to support faster action in the field.

Why We Built This

Tomato diseases can spread quickly and reduce both yield and fruit quality. Early detection makes treatment more effective, but expert support is not always available when needed. We built AI Tomato Care to make diagnosis easier and more accessible by combining image analysis with clear, action-focused recommendations.

How It Works
  • Upload: Add a clear tomato leaf image (JPG or PNG) from the Home page.
  • Detect: The system preprocesses the image and runs it through a trained CNN model to predict disease class or healthy status.
  • Results: View the predicted result with a confidence score.
  • Care Tips: Follow care guidance tailored to each detected condition.
  • History: Logged-in users can review previous scans in the History section.
Features
  • Fast image-based scanning for tomato leaves.
  • Detection of Early Blight, Late Blight, Leaf Mold, and healthy leaf condition.
  • Clear care tips linked to each detection.
  • Scan history and progress tracking for users.
  • Secure account workflow with sign-up, OTP email verification, and password reset.
  • Simple interface for scanning, care guidance, history, and account management.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI?

Accuracy is strongest when the photo is clear, well-lit, and focused on a single leaf. For the most reliable result, avoid blurry images, dark lighting, and crowded backgrounds.

Do I need an account?

You can run a quick scan without signing in. Creating an account lets you save scan history, manage your profile, and access full account settings.

What diseases are supported?

The system currently identifies four classes: Early Blight, Late Blight, Leaf Mold, and Healthy leaf condition.

How do I reset my password?

Select the Forgot Password option on the login page and submit your email address. You will receive a reset link with instructions to create a new password.
